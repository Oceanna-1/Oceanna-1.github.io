User Input - Perform Some Calculations!

Pick Up Jonks

To begin, press the space bar on your keyboard. This will cause the ball to drop. You have until the ball reaches the top of the screen again to pick up all the jonks.

Press the space bar to begin each new round. Left click on a jonk to make it disapear. Each round more jonks will be added. See how many rounds you can last!

If you miss a jonk before the ball reaches the top, the game is over!

Too hard for you? Press the easy mode button at the bottom to increase the size of the jonks.

Want to jazz things up? Double click the background to change the color!
